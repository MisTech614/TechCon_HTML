Our History

TechCon began in 2012 as a grassroots meetup of engineers, designers, and founders eager to share practical knowledge. Over the years, it has grown into a globally recognized conference known for hands-on sessions, open dialogue, and breakthrough demos that preview what’s next in technology.

Our Mission

TechCon exists to accelerate innovation through open, inclusive collaboration. We bring together practitioners from software, hardware, AI, product, and design to exchange ideas that turn into real-world impact. We prioritize community, accessibility, and rigorous, vendor-neutral learning.

  • Learn: Practical, code-heavy sessions you can apply the next day.
  • Build: Labs and workshops that produce working prototypes.
  • Connect: Mentorship and networking that outlast the conference.
